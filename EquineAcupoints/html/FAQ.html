Where do I start?
   
If the horse is not in an emergency situation, please follow the basic steps below:
    1.  Open the Bladder meridian
    2. Perform the basic assessment (advanced assessment taught in our courses)
    3.  Administer therapy to reaction/pain points. If using a Red Light Therapy device, please consult with the manufacturer on specific dosage
    4. For specific issues, please refer to the Charts section. If you are unable to locate
        a chart to your horse's specific issue, refer to the Meridian and/or Ting Point
        sections.
