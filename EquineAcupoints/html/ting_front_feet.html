SI-1 1st point of the Small Intestine Meridian and arterial vessels, especially larger ones.  Reactions may indicate :

malabsorption syndrome, foreleg, deep flexor tendon issues

TH-1 1st point of the Triple Heater meridian, skin microcirculation, mucus cavities and joint cavities. Reactions may indicate:

Recurring sinusitis

Joint fluid (serous) is too thin

Sore/cracked heels

Hoof-bend bleeders

Fetlock pain

*Reactive in horses who have/had frequent intraarticual hyaluronic acid injections.

LI1 1st point of the Large Intestine meridian.

Forelimb muscles, shoulder and colon.

Horse may have sour smelling feces and consistency of feces will vary.
