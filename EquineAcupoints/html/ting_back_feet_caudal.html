Reactions may indicate:

Headache

*Symptoms may include odd head behavior-excessive nodding, pressing head into something.

SP-1 1st Point on the Spleen Meridian.

Reactions may indicate:

Psoas muscle issues

*Recovery may be slow

KI-1 1st Point on the  Kidney Meridian.

Reactions may indicate:

Stiff and/or weak lumbar region

Stifle issues

Infertility

Unpredictable behavior

Bone spavin

Weakening of bones

*Beneficial on growing horses (age 0-4)
